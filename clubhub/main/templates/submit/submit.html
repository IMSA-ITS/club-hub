Register your event with Club Hub.

Complete the form below to enter your event into the Club Hub database. After you submit, Student Life will review your event for approval.

  • Please plan ahead and submit your poster at least two business days in advance.
  • Also, as a reminder, if you are using any images that you have not created yourself, you will need to cite the URL for where the images came from at the bottom of your poster.
  • Club Hub supports three types of slides: text-only slides, poster and text slides, and fullscreen poster slides. If you don't include a poster, Club Hub will display the information you entered in a text-only format. If you include a poster but don't select "Display poster fullscreen on Club Hub LIVE," it will split the screen between the text description and a poster to the right of it. If you select the fullscreen option, only the poster will be shown, centered, on a black background. The recommended size for fullscreen posters is 1920x1080px. Posters that will not be fullscreen should be square and between 600x600px and 1000x1000px. The poster should also be <1MB in size (try JPG compression if you need to).
  • If you have any questions, please contact Student Life.
